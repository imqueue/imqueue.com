Support questions

Is there free support?

Yes. Documentation, GitHub issues, and discussions on the @imqueue organization are open to everyone at no cost. Commercial support adds guaranteed response times and direct maintainer access on top.

What response times do you offer?

Response targets are set per agreement against your severity levels, so a production-down incident is treated differently from a general question. Tell us what you need and we'll define an SLA that fits.

Do I need a commercial license to get support?

Commercial support and the commercial license are offered together — the license covers closed-source use and indemnification, support covers the SLA and direct access. Many teams want both; tell us your situation and we'll scope it.

Can you help with architecture or migrations?

Yes. Commercial support includes design reviews, upgrade planning, and migration help from the maintainers — the kind of guidance that's hard to get from docs alone.

Do you offer indemnification?

Yes — legal indemnification is bundled with a commercial license, giving your legal team the cover a bare open-source dependency can't provide.
